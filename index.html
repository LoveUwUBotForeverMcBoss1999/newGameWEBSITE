THE MISSION

Long ago, massive underwater tunnels connected cities across the ocean floor. These submarine highways bustled with public transport until disaster struck. Now they lie abandoned, infested with deadly sharks and crumbling infrastructure.

You are the tunnel cleaner. Armed with determination and pixel-perfect precision, navigate the treacherous depths, eliminate the shark threat, and restore these vital passages for humanity.
  • Navigate claustrophobic underwater tunnels
  • Battle aggressive shark populations
  • Avoid deadly tunnel wall damage
  • Restore public transportation routes
  • Master pixel-perfect movement controls
  • Survive the crushing ocean depths
